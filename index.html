MECHANICS

01 :: THE COMMITMENT Each boarding pass carries a PDF417 barcode with the raw airline payload inside. Its keccak256 hash is stored on-chain per token. Nobody can reproduce the hash without the pass. The art is the proof.
02 :: THE WINDOW A record is filed before boarding. The purchase window opens at scheduled departure and closes at arrival. While the subject is airborne, anyone may claim the record. Wheels-down closes the file.
03 :: SEALED / DECLASSIFIED During the flight the token is SEALED: rendered from the commitment hash only, every identifying field a redaction bar. At landing it flips DECLASSIFIED: route hero, full ledger, the true barcode wall processed beyond recovery. The reveal is the landing.
04 :: UNCLAIMED = SEALED FOREVER A record nobody claims during its flight locks permanently at window close. No re-list. No re-open. Enforced in the contract, not by promise. If you were not watching while the subject flew, you missed it.
05 :: THE LEDGER Payments forward to the artist inside the buyer's own transaction; the contract holds nothing. Storage is permanent on Arweave, paid once. Maximum 12 records can ever exist. Royalties 6.9 percent.
06 :: CHAPTERS The accent color of each record derives from its real booking reference. One booking, one chapter. The data picks the palette, not the artist. Collectors can complete chapters.
